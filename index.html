About the Project

PixieFits is designed to revolutionize the way people shop for clothing and skincare. By analyzing your unique body and skin profile, we provide personalized recommendations and insights. The app goes beyond simple measurements, offering a vibrant community and curated content to empower users to express themselves with confidence.

Key Features

Personalized Analysis

Receive a detailed analysis of your body and skin profile to get tailored clothing and skincare recommendations. Our technology considers specific sizing and product compositions for the perfect match.

Community Sharing

Connect with "PixieGirls" in our community to share outfit ideas, discover new styles, and get inspiration from others with similar body types and preferences.

Trip Packing Assistant

Planning a trip? The app helps you curate the perfect wardrobe for your destination by considering the weather, activities, and duration of your visit.
