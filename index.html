About PixieFits

PixieFits is an AI-powered personal style and beauty assistant designed to make shopping more personal and more accurate. Instead of relying on trends or influencer recommendations, PixieFits helps users understand what actually fits their body and skin.

The app combines body analysis, skin insights, and ingredient-level product understanding to guide smarter decisions in fashion and skincare.
