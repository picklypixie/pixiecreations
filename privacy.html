Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

PixieFits (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the PixieFits iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email Address: Used for login, authentication, and account management.
  • User-Uploaded Photos: Outfit photos, body photos for AI analysis, and clothing label images for OCR measurement extraction.
  • User-Generated Content: Comments, likes, saved outfits, and posts in the community feed.
  • Anonymous Usage Data: Crash logs, device information, and interactions used to improve app performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Account login and authentication
  • AI fit analysis & measurement recommendations
  • Displaying outfit feed content
  • Improving performance, stability, and user experience
  • Responding to support requests

Your information is not used for:

  • Third-party advertising
  • Tracking across apps or websites
  • Selling or sharing data with external companies

3. Data Storage & Security

PixieFits uses Google Firebase for secure cloud storage, including encrypted Firestore databases and secure Storage buckets. Data is protected through standard industry practices, including HTTPS encryption and authentication rules.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third-party organizations. Data is only used internally to support PixieFits features and improvements.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You may request:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Complete deletion of your account and data

To request deletion or updates, contact: picklyrecipes@gmail.com

6. Children’s Privacy

PixieFits is not intended for children under age 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under this age.

7. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant updates will be communicated within the app.
